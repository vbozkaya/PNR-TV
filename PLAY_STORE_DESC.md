## 📄 Long Description (Max 4000 Chars)

### English Version

**Experience your media collection like never before on the big screen.**

PNR TV is a professional media player application optimized specifically for Android TV interfaces. It delivers a smooth, intuitive, and cinematic experience for your video content, allowing you to manage and play your playlists with ease.

**Key Features:**

📺 **Native TV Experience**
Designed from the ground up for large screens. Enjoy a fully native Android TV user interface (Leanback) that is perfectly navigable with your standard remote control.

🎬 **Smart Library Management**
Organize your network streams efficiently:
- **Live Streams:** Fast channel zapping and stream management.
- **Movies (VOD):** Browse your video library with categories, detailed metadata, and posters.
- **Series:** Keep track of your TV shows with season and episode organization.

🚀 **Powerful Playback Engine**
- Built on a robust media player architecture.
- Supports a wide range of video formats and codecs (MKV, MP4, TS, HLS, DASH, etc.).
- Advanced support for multi-audio tracks and subtitles.

👤 **Personalized Experience**
- **Multi-Profile:** Create separate watch profiles for family members.
- **Favorites:** Quickly access your most-loved content.
- **Resume Watching:** The app remembers exactly where you left off.
- **Watch History:** Easily find what you watched recently.

**Note:**
PNR TV is purely a media player. It **does not** contain any pre-installed channels, streams, or video content. Users must provide their own content/playlists.

**Disclaimer:**
- PNR TV does not supply or include any media or content.
- Users must provide their own content from their own storage or playlists.
- PNR TV has no affiliation with any third-party content provider.
- We do not endorse the streaming of copyright-protected material without permission of the copyright holder.
