PNR TV Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

PNR TV - Smart Media Player ("we", "our", "app") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy describes our data collection, use, and protection practices.

By using this application, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

2. Data Collected

2.1. User Accounts

The app stores the following information locally on your device to connect to your media service provider or playlist source:

  • Username: Your media service account username
  • Password: Your media service account password (stored encrypted)
  • Server URL/DNS: Media server address or playlist URL (stored encrypted)
  • Account Name: Name you use to identify your account

Important: All sensitive data (passwords and server information) is encrypted using Android's secure encryption mechanism and stored only on your device. This information is never sent to our servers or third parties.

2.2. Usage Data

The app stores the following data locally to improve user experience:

  • Favorites: Your favorite channels and content
  • Recently Watched: Record of recently watched content
  • Playback Positions: Resume positions for watched content
  • Viewer Profiles: Separate viewer profiles for each user account

This data is stored only on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2.3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for debugging and performance improvement. These services may collect the following information:

  • Device Information: Device model, Android version, manufacturer information
  • App Information: App version, build type
  • Crash Reports: App crashes and errors (does not contain personal information)
  • Usage Statistics: General usage metrics (anonymized)

Important: Firebase services do not collect usernames, passwords, DNS, or other personal information (PII). All data is anonymized and used for aggregate statistical purposes.

2.4. Third-Party Services

The app uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to provide movie and series information. Requests sent to TMDB only contain content search and detail information; your personal information is not sent.

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Connecting to your media service provider or playlist source and playing content
  • Storing your favorites and watch history
  • Enabling resume from where you left off
  • Detecting and fixing app errors
  • Improving app performance

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1. Local Data Storage

All user data (account information, favorites, watch history) is stored only on your device in Android Room database. This data:

  • Sensitive information (passwords, DNS) is stored encrypted.
  • Is never sent over the internet.
  • Remains on your device until you delete it.

4.2. Data Security

We take the following measures to ensure the security of your data:

  • Sensitive data is encrypted using Android Security Crypto.
  • Secure connections are ensured through network security configuration.
  • Code obfuscation is applied with ProGuard.
  • PII (Personally Identifiable Information) protection is active in Firebase services.

5. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties. The app:

  • Never sends your user accounts, passwords, or server information to any server
  • Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics only collect anonymous usage statistics and error reports
  • TMDB API is only used for content search and detail information

6. Data Deletion

All your data is stored on your device and under your control:

  • You can delete user accounts from app settings.
  • You can clear cache from app settings.
  • All data is deleted when you uninstall the app from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will immediately delete that data.

8. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes take effect when published on this page. We may send notifications within the app for significant changes.

You can see the most current version by checking the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
